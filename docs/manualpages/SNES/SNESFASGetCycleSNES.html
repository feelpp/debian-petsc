SNESFASGetCycleSNES

 Gets the SNES corresponding to a particular level of the FAS hierarchy.

Synopsis

#include "petscsnesfas.h"  
PetscErrorCode SNESFASGetCycleSNES(SNES snes,PetscInt level,SNES *lsnes)

Input Parameters

snes - the multigrid context level - the level to get
lsnes - whether to use the nonlinear smoother or not

Keywords

FAS, MG, set, cycles, Gauss-Seidel, multigrid

See Also

SNESFASSetLevels(), SNESFASGetLevels()
